# Data Processing Agreement

**Effective date:** 1 January 2026
**Last updated:** 1 January 2026

---

## 1. Introduction

This Data Processing Agreement ("DPA") forms part of the Terms of Use between **Marketer Pro Office** ("Processor" or "we") and the user ("Controller" or "you") and is required under Article 28 of the General Data Protection Regulation (EU) 2016/679 ("GDPR").

This DPA applies where Marketer Pro Office processes personal data on your behalf as part of delivering the Service.

---

## 2. Definitions

| Term | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Personal Data** | Any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person, as defined under GDPR Article 4(1) |
| **Processing** | Any operation performed on Personal Data, including collection, storage, use, and deletion |
| **Controller** | The user who determines the purposes and means of processing Personal Data |
| **Processor** | Marketer Pro Office, which processes Personal Data on behalf of the Controller |
| **Sub-processor** | A third party engaged by Marketer Pro Office to process Personal Data |
| **Data Subject** | The natural person to whom Personal Data relates |

---

## 3. Scope of Processing

### 3.1 Subject Matter
Marketer Pro Office processes Personal Data for the purpose of providing AI-powered marketing content generation, scheduling, analytics, and brand management services.

### 3.2 Nature of Processing
Storage, retrieval, transmission to AI sub-processors for content generation, aggregation for analytics, and deletion upon request.

### 3.3 Types of Personal Data Processed

| Category | Examples |
|---|---|
| Account identifiers | Email address, user ID |
| Brand data | Brand name, logo, colour palettes |
| Audience data | Audience demographic and psychographic traits you define |
| Content data | AI-generated copy, captions, and prompts you submit |
| Usage data | Feature interactions, generation counts, session metadata |

### 3.4 Categories of Data Subjects
Users of the Controller's account; audience profile data does not relate to identified individuals and is defined at a categorical level by the Controller.

### 3.5 Duration
For the duration of the active subscription, plus any retention period specified in the Privacy Policy.

---

## 4. Obligations of the Processor

Marketer Pro Office shall:

1. Process Personal Data only on documented instructions from the Controller (i.e. the user's actions within the Service), unless required to do so by applicable law
2. Ensure that persons authorised to process Personal Data are bound by confidentiality obligations
3. Implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to ensure a level of security appropriate to the risk, as described in our [Security Policy](./security.md)
4. Not engage a Sub-processor without prior general or specific authorisation from the Controller, subject to Section 6 below
5. Assist the Controller in fulfilling obligations to respond to Data Subject rights requests
6. Delete or return all Personal Data to the Controller upon termination of the Service, at the Controller's choice, and delete existing copies unless retention is required by law
7. Make available to the Controller all information necessary to demonstrate compliance with this DPA
8. Not transfer Personal Data outside the European Economic Area without appropriate safeguards in place

---

## 5. Obligations of the Controller

The Controller shall:

1. Ensure there is a lawful basis for processing Personal Data using the Service
2. Ensure Data Subjects have been informed of the processing as required by GDPR Articles 13 and 14
3. Not instruct Marketer Pro Office to process Personal Data in a manner that would violate applicable law
4. Notify Marketer Pro Office promptly of any Data Subject rights requests or regulatory enquiries relating to Personal Data processed through the Service

---

The Controller grants general authorisation for Marketer Pro Office to engage the following categories of Sub-processors. We maintain a current list of named Sub-processors and will notify the Controller of any changes with at least 30 days' notice.

### 6.1 Current Sub-processors

| Sub-processor | Category | Purpose | Location |
|---|---|---|---|
| Supabase | Cloud infrastructure | Database hosting, authentication, storage | EU / US (Standard Contractual Clauses apply) |
| AI content provider (e.g. Anthropic, OpenAI) | AI processing | Content generation from user prompts | US (Standard Contractual Clauses apply) |

### 6.2 Constraints on Sub-processors
All Sub-processors are bound by data processing agreements that impose obligations equivalent to those in this DPA. We confirm that no Sub-processor is permitted to use Personal Data submitted through the Service to train their AI models without explicit consent.

---

## 7. International Transfers

Where Personal Data is transferred to a country outside the EEA that does not have an adequacy decision, Marketer Pro Office ensures appropriate safeguards are in place, including Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission.

---

## 8. Security

Marketer Pro Office implements the technical and organisational measures described in our [Security Policy](./security.md), including:

- Encryption of Personal Data in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
- Access controls and least-privilege principles
- Regular security assessments
- Incident response procedures

---

## 9. Personal Data Breaches

In the event of a Personal Data breach that is likely to result in a risk to the rights and freedoms of Data Subjects, Marketer Pro Office shall notify the Controller without undue delay and in any event within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach, providing sufficient information to allow the Controller to fulfil its notification obligations under GDPR Article 33.

---

## 10. Data Subject Rights

Marketer Pro Office will assist the Controller in responding to Data Subject rights requests (access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, objection) within the Service where technically feasible. Controllers can initiate data export and deletion requests at **Settings → Privacy → Data & Exports**.

---

## 11. Audit Rights

The Controller may, upon reasonable notice of not less than 30 days and no more than once per calendar year, request an audit of Marketer Pro Office's data processing activities relevant to this DPA. Marketer Pro Office may satisfy this obligation by providing relevant third-party audit reports (e.g. SOC 2) or certifications.

---

## 12. Term and Termination

This DPA remains in force for as long as Marketer Pro Office processes Personal Data on behalf of the Controller. Upon termination of the Service, Marketer Pro Office will delete Personal Data within 90 days unless a longer retention period is required by law.
