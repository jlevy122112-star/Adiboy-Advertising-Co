# Acceptable Use Policy

**Effective date:** 1 January 2026
**Last updated:** 1 January 2026

---

## 1. Purpose

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") defines what is and is not permitted when using Marketer Pro Office ("the Service"). It applies to all users, including free, Pro, and Enterprise subscribers.

By using the Service, you agree to comply with this AUP. Violation may result in suspension or permanent termination of your account without refund.

---

## 2. Permitted Uses

The Service is designed for legitimate marketing and content creation purposes. Permitted uses include:

- Creating and scheduling social media content for brands you own or are authorised to manage
- Generating marketing copy, captions, hashtags, and creative briefs using the AI content engine
- Managing brand identity, audience profiles, and content calendars
- Publishing content to social platforms you own or have explicit authorisation to post to
- Analysing performance metrics for your own campaigns
- Collaborating with team members within an Enterprise organisation account

---

## 3. Prohibited Content

You must not use the Service to generate, store, schedule, or publish content that:

### 3.1 Is Unlawful
- Violates any applicable local, national, or international law or regulation
- Infringes the intellectual property rights, privacy rights, or other legal rights of any person
- Constitutes defamation, libel, or slander
- Facilitates or encourages any illegal activity

### 3.2 Is Harmful or Abusive
- Constitutes harassment, intimidation, bullying, or targeted abuse of any individual or group
- Promotes, glorifies, or incites violence, self-harm, or harm to others
- Contains graphic or gratuitous violent imagery
- Is designed to exploit, harm, or endanger minors in any way
- Constitutes non-consensual intimate imagery ("revenge porn") or sexual exploitation

### 3.3 Is Discriminatory or Hateful
- Promotes hatred, discrimination, or prejudice based on race, ethnicity, national origin, religion, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, age, or any other protected characteristic
- Uses slurs or dehumanising language targeting any group

### 3.4 Is Deceptive or Manipulative
- Is intentionally false, misleading, or deceptive in a way that could cause harm
- Constitutes disinformation, propaganda, or coordinated inauthentic behaviour
- Impersonates any real person, organisation, or brand without authorisation
- Falsely implies endorsement by a public figure or organisation
- Constitutes spam, unsolicited commercial messaging, or phishing

### 3.5 Violates Platform Rules
- Violates the terms of service, community guidelines, or advertising policies of any social platform to which you publish (including Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, LinkedIn, and X)
- Attempts to game platform algorithms in ways that those platforms prohibit

### 3.6 Is Adult or Age-Restricted
- Contains sexually explicit or pornographic material
- Promotes adult products or services in a manner that would be prohibited by the relevant app store or social platform

### 3.7 Endangers Public Health or Safety
- Promotes dangerous, unproven, or fraudulent health products or medical claims
- Constitutes anti-vaccine misinformation or other public health misinformation
- Promotes the sale of controlled substances, weapons, or other prohibited goods

## 4. Prohibited System Uses

In addition to prohibited content, you must not:

- Attempt to reverse-engineer, decompile, or extract the source code of the Service
- Use automated scripts, bots, or scrapers to access the Service beyond what is expressly permitted by our API
- Attempt to gain unauthorised access to any part of the Service, its infrastructure, or other users' accounts
- Use the Service to test, develop, or train competing AI systems
- Resell, sublicence, or commercially redistribute the Service or its AI output without our prior written consent
- Generate content at a volume designed to overwhelm or abuse our AI infrastructure (rate limits apply)
- Attempt to bypass safety filters, content moderation systems, or rate limits in the Service
- Share account credentials with unauthorised users

The AI content engine in Marketer Pro Office generates content based on your inputs. You are solely responsible for:

- Reviewing all AI-generated content before publishing
- Ensuring AI-generated content complies with this AUP, our Terms of Use, and applicable law

- Any consequences arising from content you publish using the Service

We reserve the right to implement content filters and to refuse to generate content that violates this AUP. We are not liable for AI-generated content that you choose to publish.

We reserve the right to take immediate action without warning for serious violations, including those involving illegal content, child safety, or imminent harm.
