# The Desk Workspace

The Desk is your permanent home base in Marketer Pro Office. Everything you need to plan, generate, and schedule content lives here. It is made up of five zones.

---

## Zone 1 — Workspace Header

The top bar of the Desk. Always visible.

### Brand Switcher
The brand name displayed in the top-left is your currently active workspace. Tap it to:
- Switch between existing brands
- Create a new brand (tap **+ New Brand**)

All tasks, generated content, and scheduled posts are scoped to the active brand. Switching brands immediately updates the Planner Surface and Calendar Drawer to show that brand's data.

### Level Badge
The gold **LV.X** badge on the right of the header shows your current level and XP progress bar. Hover or tap it to see:
- Current XP total
- XP needed for the next level
- Current streak

### Calendar Toggle
The calendar icon opens and closes the Calendar Drawer. On tablet and desktop, the Calendar Drawer is always visible alongside the Planner.

### Notifications
The bell icon shows unread notifications — upcoming scheduled posts, XP milestones, lootbox rewards, and system messages.

### Profile
Your avatar in the top-right corner opens your account menu — access Settings, the Creator Hub, and sign out.

---

## Zone 2 — Tools Panel

The left panel contains navigation to every tool and scene in the app.

| Tool | Unlocked at | What it does |
|---|---|---|
| **Vault Generator** | Level 1 | Opens the Generator Form to start a content generation cycle |
| **Campaigns** | Level 1 | Create and manage campaigns to group content |
| **Audiences** | Level 1 | Define and manage audience profiles |
| **Observatory** | Level 3 | Analytics and performance insights |
| **Asset Vault** | Level 1 | Your media library of uploaded brand assets |
| **Templates** | Level 2 | Reusable content templates |
| **Integrations** | Level 4 | Connect and manage social platform accounts |
| **Settings** | Always | Account, billing, privacy, and app preferences |

Tools with a padlock icon are locked until you reach the required level. Your current level is shown in the header.

**On mobile:** The Tools Panel slides in from the left. Tap the panel icon in the Quick Actions Bar or the top-left header icon to open it. Tap outside the panel or swipe left to close it.

**On tablet/desktop:** The Tools Panel is always visible on the left side of the screen.

---

## Zone 3 — Planner Surface

The main centre area of the Desk. Shows your tasks for today.

### Today's Tasks
Tasks are things you need to do for your active brand today — write a brief, review a post, set up a campaign. They are created by you and optionally linked to a campaign.

**Task statuses:**
- **To Do** — not yet started
- **In Progress** — actively being worked on
- **Done** — completed (shown with strikethrough and dimmed)

**Priority badges:**
- **HIGH** — red badge
- **MED** — amber badge
- **LOW** — grey badge

### Adding a Task
Tap the **+ Add task** button at the bottom of the Planner to add a new task inline. Type the title and press Enter or tap **Add**. Press Escape to cancel.

### Completing a Task
Tap the circle icon to the left of a task to toggle it between To Do and Done. The completion percentage and progress bar at the top update in real time.

### Task Details
Tap the chevron (→) on the right of a task to open the full task detail view where you can set priority, due date, platform, and description.

### Streak and Completion
The top of the Planner shows:
- **Flame icon + number** — your current daily generation streak
- **Trend icon + percentage** — today's task completion rate

---

## Zone 4 — Calendar Drawer

The right panel shows your content calendar.

### Navigating Months
Use the **← →** arrows at the top of the Calendar Drawer to move between months.

### Reading the Calendar
Each day cell shows coloured dots representing scheduled posts:
- **Gold dot** — scheduled post
- **Green dot** — successfully published post
- **Grey dot** — draft post
- **Red dot** — failed post

### Selecting a Day
Tap any day to see that day's posts listed below the calendar grid. Each post shows the platform icon, caption preview, and scheduled time.

### Scheduling a New Post
Tap the **+** icon next to the date label above the post list to schedule a new post for that day. This opens the Scheduler Tower.

**On mobile:** The Calendar Drawer slides in from the right. Tap the calendar icon in the Quick Actions Bar or the Workspace Header to open it. Tap outside or swipe right to close.

---

## Zone 5 — Quick Actions Bar

The bottom navigation bar. Always visible.

| Button | Action |
|---|---|
| **Panel icon** | Open/close Tools Panel (mobile only) |
| **New Post** | Create a new post manually |
| **Generate (gold wand)** | Open the Vault Generator — the primary action |
| **Campaign** | Quick-access to Campaigns |
| **Calendar** | Open/close Calendar Drawer (mobile only) |

The **Generate** button is the centrepiece — it sits elevated above the bar with a gold glow. The number badge on it shows your total generation count for the current session.

## Keyboard Shortcuts (Tablet/Desktop)

| Shortcut | Action |
|---|---|
| `G` | Open Vault Generator |
| `T` | Focus task input |
| `[` | Toggle Tools Panel |
| `]` | Toggle Calendar Drawer |
| `Esc` | Close any open panel or modal |
