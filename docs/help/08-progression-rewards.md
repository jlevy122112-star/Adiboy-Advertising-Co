# Progression & Rewards

Marketer Pro Office has a full progression system that rewards you for using the app consistently and effectively. The more you generate, schedule, and publish, the more your office evolves.

---

## Experience Points (XP)

XP is the currency of progression. You earn XP by completing actions in the app.

### XP Sources

| Action | XP awarded |
|---|---|
| Complete a generation cycle | 100 XP |
| Complete Brand Identity Chamber | 50 XP |
| Complete Audience Arena | 50 XP |
| Create a new audience profile | 25 XP |
| Confirm a scheduled post | 25 XP |
| Successfully publish a post | 50 XP |
| Complete all tasks on the Planner | 30 XP |
| Maintain a daily streak | Streak bonus (see below) |
| Open a Lootbox | 10–200 XP (bonus, inside the lootbox) |

### XP Multipliers
Your XP multiplier increases with your Prestige Rank (see below). At Prestige 1, all XP awards are multiplied by 1.5×. At Prestige 2, by 2×.

---

## Levels

XP accumulates into levels. Each level requires more XP than the last.

| Level range | XP per level |
|---|---|
| 1–5 | 1,000 XP |
| 6–10 | 2,000 XP |
| 11–15 | 3,500 XP |
| 16–19 | 5,000 XP |
| 20 | Prestige unlock |

Your current level and XP progress are shown in the **Level Badge** in the Workspace Header. The mini XP bar fills as you earn XP toward the next level.

### Level Unlocks

| Level | Unlock |
|---|---|
| 1 | Vault Generator, Brand Identity Chamber, Audience Arena, Planner |
| 2 | Templates library |
| 3 | Observatory (Analytics) |
| 4 | Social Integrations |
| 5 | Office Upgrade — Tier 2 visual |
| 7 | Audience Arena — advanced traits |
| 10 | Future Performance Telescope (Observatory) |
| 15 | Office Upgrade — Tier 3 visual |
| 20 | Prestige unlock |

## Streaks

A **streak** is the number of consecutive days you have completed at least one generation.

Your streak is shown with a 🔥 flame icon in the Planner Surface header and in your Creator Hub profile.

### Streak Bonus
Each day of your streak adds a bonus to your XP awards:
- 3-day streak: +10% XP on all awards
- 7-day streak: +25% XP on all awards
- 14-day streak: +40% XP on all awards
- 30-day streak: +60% XP on all awards

### Losing a Streak
If you do not complete a generation on a given day, your streak resets to 0. The streak counter updates at midnight in your local timezone.

> **Streak freeze (Pro):** Pro users get one streak freeze per month. A streak freeze automatically activates on a missed day to protect your streak. Manage streak freezes in **Settings → Progression**.

## Lootboxes

Lootboxes are reward drops that appear after significant milestones — level-ups, streak milestones, and specific achievement unlocks.

### Opening a Lootbox
When a lootbox is available, a notification appears and the **Lootbox** button appears in the Workspace Header. Tap it to open the **Content Forge Scene** where the lootbox reveal plays.

### What's Inside

| Reward type | What you get |
|---|---|
| **XP Boost** | Bonus XP added directly to your total |
| **Cosmetic** | A new visual element for your office (desk skin, particle effect, ambient theme) |
| **Scene Unlock** | Early access to a VR scene before the required level |
| **Template** | A premium content template added to your Templates library |
| **Badge** | A display badge for your Creator Hub profile |

### Lootbox Rarity

Each lootbox has a rarity tier that determines the quality of rewards inside:

| Rarity | Probability | Minimum reward |
|---|---|---|
| Common | 60% | 50 XP or standard cosmetic |
| Rare | 25% | 150 XP or premium cosmetic |
| Epic | 12% | 300 XP or scene unlock |
| Legendary | 3% | 500 XP + exclusive cosmetic + badge |

## Office Evolution

Your office environment visually upgrades as your level increases. Each level brings micro-changes — lighting, furniture, ambient effects, particles — that accumulate into dramatically different environments at tier thresholds.

| Level | Office tier | Visual description |
|---|---|---|
| 1–4 | Starter Office | Bare desk, dim lighting, minimal atmosphere |
| 5–9 | Junior Suite | Better lighting, desk accessories, subtle particles |
| 10–14 | Senior Suite | Rich ambient glow, animated background, more particles |
| 15–19 | Executive Floor | Full atmospheric effects, premium desk skin |
| 20 | C-Suite Penthouse | Maximum visual fidelity, prestige effects |

Office visual changes happen automatically when you reach each level. You can preview upcoming office levels in the **Creator Hub → Office Evolution** section.

### Cosmetic Customisation
Cosmetics earned from lootboxes can be applied to customise individual elements of your office. Apply cosmetics at **Settings → Office → Customise**.

---

## Prestige

At Level 20, you unlock **Prestige** — the ability to reset your level back to 1 in exchange for permanent upgrades.

Prestige rewards include:
- A permanent XP multiplier (1.5× at Prestige 1, 2× at Prestige 2)
- An exclusive Prestige badge displayed in your Creator Hub
- A special prestige particle effect in your office
- Access to the **Prestige Sanctum** VR scene

You retain all cosmetics, lootbox rewards, achievements, and office upgrades when you prestige. Only your XP and level reset.

## Achievements

Achievements are one-time milestone awards that appear in your Creator Hub profile.

Examples:

| Achievement | How to earn |
|---|---|
| First Generation | Complete your first Vault Generator cycle |
| Brand Architect | Complete the Brand Identity Chamber |
| Audience Intel | Complete the Audience Arena |
| 7-Day Warrior | Maintain a 7-day streak |
| Legendary Pull | Receive a Legendary artifact |
| First Publish | Successfully publish a post to a connected platform |
| Century | Complete 100 generations |
| Prestige I | Reach Prestige Rank 1 |

Achievements are displayed in your Creator Hub profile with their rarity badge. Legendary achievements have an animated gold shimmer.
