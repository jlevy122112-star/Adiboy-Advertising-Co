## Pro Plan

**Price:** $19.99/month or $179.99/year (save 25%)

The Pro plan is designed for individual marketers, freelancers, and small business owners who use content generation as a regular part of their workflow.

Key benefits over Free:
- Unlimited generations — no monthly cap
- Unlimited artifact retention — your entire vault history, forever
- All 5 social platform integrations
- Observatory analytics
- Templates library
- 1 streak freeze per month

---

## Enterprise Plan

**Price:** Contact sales at enterprise@marketerprooffice.com

Enterprise is designed for agencies, marketing teams, and larger organisations. It adds team collaboration on top of everything in Pro.

Key benefits over Pro:
- Unlimited brands
- Team organisation — invite colleagues, share a vault
- Shared artifact vault — team members can access each other's generated content
- Unlimited brands across the organisation
- 3 streak freezes per month per member
- Priority support with a dedicated response SLA

To set up an Enterprise account, contact **enterprise@marketerprooffice.com**.

## Upgrading to Pro

### Via iOS (App Store)
1. Go to **Settings → Billing → Upgrade to Pro**
2. Select monthly or annual
3. Confirm with Face ID / Touch ID or your Apple ID password
4. Your plan upgrades instantly

### Via Android (Google Play)
1. Go to **Settings → Billing → Upgrade to Pro**
2. Select monthly or annual
3. Confirm with your Google account
4. Your plan upgrades instantly

### Proration
If you upgrade mid-billing period (Free → Pro), you are charged the Pro price immediately and your billing date resets to today. There is no proration for the unused portion of a Free period since Free has no charge.

---

## Cancelling Your Subscription

Cancelling stops future charges but keeps your Pro access until the end of the current billing period.

### iOS
**Settings (iPhone) → Your Name → Subscriptions → Marketer Pro Office → Cancel Subscription**

### Android
**Google Play Store → Menu → Subscriptions → Marketer Pro Office → Cancel**

After cancellation takes effect, your account reverts to the Free plan. Artifacts older than 30 days become inaccessible (but are not deleted for 90 days — resubscribing restores access).

## Billing Receipts

Receipts are managed by the App Store or Google Play and sent to your Apple ID or Google account email. For Enterprise direct billing, receipts are emailed to your account email after each charge.

To view your billing history: **Settings → Billing → Billing History**

## Free Trial

New users may be eligible for a 7-day free trial of Pro. The trial appears on the Upgrade screen if available for your account. You will not be charged if you cancel before the trial ends.

## FAQs

**Can I switch from monthly to annual?**
Yes. Go to **Settings → Billing → Change Plan** and select the annual option. You are charged the annual price immediately and receive a credit for the unused days on your current monthly plan.

**What happens to my artifacts if I downgrade?**
Your artifacts are not deleted. Artifacts older than 30 days become inaccessible while on the Free plan. They are retained for 90 days — resubscribing to Pro within that window restores full access.

**Can I have multiple Pro accounts?**
Each account requires its own subscription. To share access with a team, use the Enterprise plan with a team organisation.

**I was charged but didn't intend to upgrade.**
Contact Apple (reportaproblem.apple.com), Google (play.google.com/store/account/subscriptions), or our billing team at billing@marketerprooffice.com depending on how you were charged.
