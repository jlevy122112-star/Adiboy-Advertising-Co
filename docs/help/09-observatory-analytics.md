# Observatory — Analytics

The Observatory is your analytics command center. It provides a holographic view of your content performance across all connected social platforms.

**Access:** Tools Panel → Observatory (unlocks at Level 3)

---

## Overview Metrics

The top of the Observatory shows four key performance indicators for your active brand:

| Metric | What it measures |
|---|---|
| **Total Posts** | The number of posts published through Marketer Pro Office across all platforms in the selected period |
| **Total Reach** | The cumulative number of unique accounts that saw your content |
| **Engagement Rate** | Average (likes + comments + shares + saves) ÷ reach × 100, across all posts |
| **Growth** | Percentage change in total reach compared to the previous equivalent period |

---

## Weekly Reach Chart

The bar chart below the metric cards shows your daily reach for the last 7 days. Each bar represents one day (Monday through Sunday).

- Bars use a holographic teal gradient
- Bars animate upward on load
- Hover or tap a bar to see the exact reach figure for that day

The chart is useful for identifying which days of the week your content performs best, which informs your scheduling strategy.

---

## Platform Breakdown

The platform breakdown section shows the percentage of your total reach that came from each connected platform.

Progress bars animate sequentially on load. Use this to understand which platforms are driving the most reach for your brand and where to focus your generation efforts.

---

## Period Selection

By default, the Observatory shows the last 7 days. Tap the period selector to change:

| Period | What it covers |
|---|---|
| 7 days | Last 7 calendar days |
| 30 days | Last 30 calendar days |
| 90 days | Last 90 calendar days |

> **Note:** Analytics data is only available for posts published through Marketer Pro Office to connected platforms. Posts published directly on the platforms or through other tools are not tracked.

---

## Future Performance Telescope

The Future Performance Telescope is an upcoming Pro feature (unlocks at Level 10) that uses your historical data to project likely performance for your next scheduled posts.

It will show:
- Predicted reach range for your next 7 days of scheduled posts
- Optimal posting windows based on your engagement history
- Suggested adjustments to your scheduling pattern

This section shows as locked until you reach Level 10.

---

## Data Availability

Analytics data becomes available after your first connected platform publish. The system collects engagement data from each platform's API at 6-hour intervals.

| Platform | Data available | Delay |
|---|---|---|
| Instagram | Reach, likes, comments, saves | Up to 6 hours |
| Facebook | Reach, reactions, shares | Up to 6 hours |
| LinkedIn | Impressions, clicks, reactions | Up to 24 hours |
| TikTok | Views, likes, comments, shares | Up to 12 hours |
| X | Impressions, likes, retweets | Up to 6 hours |

---

## Tips

- **Check the Observatory weekly.** A weekly analytics review helps you notice patterns — which content types generate the most reach, which days perform best, which platforms are growing.
- **Compare periods.** Switch between 7-day and 30-day views to distinguish one-off spikes from genuine trends.
- **Let the data guide your generation briefs.** If LinkedIn is consistently outperforming Instagram for reach, try generating more LinkedIn-first content.
- **Engagement rate matters more than reach.** A smaller but highly engaged audience is more valuable than a large disengaged one. Focus on the engagement rate trend over time.
