# Creator Hub

The Creator Hub is your personal marketer identity page. It shows your rank, progression stats, achievement collection, office evolution status, and unlocked scenes — all in one place.

**Access:** Profile avatar in the Workspace Header → Creator Hub, or Tools Panel → Creator Hub

---

## Your Rank

Your rank title is displayed at the top of the Creator Hub. It changes as you reach new Prestige levels.

| Prestige Rank | Title |
|---|---|
| 0 (no prestige) | Apprentice Marketer |
| 1 | Junior Strategist |
| 2 | Senior Strategist |
| 3 | Campaign Director |
| 4 | Brand Architect |
| 5 | Marketing Commander |

Your rank title is visible to other users if you share your Creator Hub profile (a future feature).

---

## Progression Stats

The stats row shows four key numbers at a glance:

| Stat | What it shows |
|---|---|
| **Level** | Your current progression level (1–20, resets at Prestige) |
| **Streak** | Current consecutive-day generation streak |
| **Best** | Your longest streak ever |
| **Generated** | Total number of generation cycles you have completed |

---

## Office Evolution

The Office Evolution section shows:

- Your current office level and what tier it is
- A progress bar showing progress toward the next office upgrade
- A message telling you which level triggers the next visual upgrade
- A pill grid showing all 9 VR scenes — locked scenes show a padlock icon, unlocked scenes are highlighted

To see what each office tier looks like in advance, tap **Preview next level** (Pro feature).

---

## Achievements

Your achievement collection is displayed as a scrollable list below the office section.

Each achievement shows:
- An icon
- Title and description
- Rarity badge (Common / Rare / Epic / Legendary)
- Lock icon if not yet unlocked

Locked achievements are greyed out but still visible so you know what to work toward. Legendary achievements have an animated gold shimmer when unlocked.

### Achievement Progress
For achievements with a progress component (e.g. "Complete 100 generations"), a progress bar shows your current progress toward unlocking it. Tap an achievement to see the full details and progress.

## Prestige Badge

If you have reached Prestige Rank 1 or higher, a prestige badge is displayed next to your rank title. The badge design changes at each prestige level and is permanently displayed on your profile.

---

## Tips

- **Check the Creator Hub for achievement hints.** Locked achievements tell you exactly what action to take. Use them as goals to guide your app usage.
- **The streak stat is your most important number.** Consistent daily usage is how you level up fastest. Protect your streak.
- **Prestige is worth it.** The XP multiplier from Prestige 1 (1.5×) means you level back up significantly faster the second time.
