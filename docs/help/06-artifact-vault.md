# Artifact Vault

The Artifact Vault is your permanent library of everything the AI has ever generated for you. Every output from the Vault Generator is automatically saved here as a classified artifact.

**Access:** Tools Panel → Asset Vault → Artifact Vault tab, or from the Presentation Chamber after a generation.

---

## What Are Artifacts?

Artifacts are the individual outputs of a generation cycle. Each generation produces multiple artifacts across different types:

| Artifact type | Contents |
|---|---|
| **Copy Dossier** | 3–5 full-length copy variations |
| **Caption File** | Platform-optimised captions for each selected platform |
| **Hashtag Set** | Curated hashtag groups — broad reach, niche, and branded |
| **Variations** | Alternative angles, tones, and framings of the same brief |
| **Visual Assets** | Image generation prompts or generated images (Pro) |

---

## Artifact Rarity

Every artifact is assigned a rarity based on the quality score of the generation. Rarity is determined by AI confidence, prompt specificity, and brand alignment score.

| Rarity | Visual | What it means |
|---|---|---|
| **Common** | Grey border | Standard quality generation |
| **Rare** | Blue glow border | High-confidence output, strong brand alignment |
| **Epic** | Purple glow border | Exceptional output with multiple strong variations |
| **Legendary** | Gold shimmer border | Near-perfect generation — highly specific, on-brand, platform-optimised |

> **Tip:** Legendary artifacts are rare by design. They appear when your brand identity is fully set up, your audience is well-defined, and your brief is highly specific. The more detail you put in, the higher the rarity ceiling.

## Browsing the Vault

### Filter Bar
The filter chips at the top of the Vault let you narrow by artifact type:

- **ALL** — shows everything
- **COPY** — Copy Dossier artifacts only
- **CAPTIONS** — Caption File artifacts only
- **HASHTAGS** — Hashtag Set artifacts only
- **VARIANTS** — Variations artifacts only
- **IMAGES** — Visual Asset artifacts only

### Artifact Cards
Each card in the grid shows:
- Artifact type icon
- Rarity badge (top right)
- Content preview (first 3 lines)
- Legendary shimmer animation (for legendary artifacts)

### Artifact Detail
Tap any artifact card to open the detail drawer from the bottom of the screen. The detail view shows:
- Full content — scroll to read all variations
- Rarity badge and type label
- **Use Artifact** — copies the content or opens the post composer
- **Schedule** — opens the Scheduler Tower for this artifact

Tap outside the drawer or the × to close it.

---

## Using an Artifact

### Use Artifact
Tapping **Use Artifact** copies the primary content to your clipboard and opens the post composer pre-filled with the artifact content. You can edit before publishing.

### Schedule
Tapping **Schedule** opens the [Scheduler Tower](./05-calendar-scheduling.md) with this artifact pre-selected. Choose your time and confirm.

---

## Searching and Sorting

Use the **search bar** at the top of the Artifact Vault to find artifacts by keyword — searches across all content within the artifact.

**Sort options** (tap the sort icon):
- Newest first (default)
- Oldest first
- Rarity (Legendary → Common)
- Type

---

## Artifact Retention

Artifacts are retained for the following periods depending on your plan:

| Plan | Artifact retention |
|---|---|
| Free | Last 30 days |
| Pro | Unlimited |
| Enterprise | Unlimited + team shared vault |

When an artifact reaches the retention limit on the Free plan, it is permanently deleted. Upgrade to Pro to keep all artifacts indefinitely.

---

## The Content Forge Scene

After each generation, the **Content Forge Scene** briefly plays before the Presentation Chamber. This represents the AI "forging" your artifacts — it's a visual wrapper for the lootbox reveal that assigns rarity to each artifact. The forge scene's animation length corresponds to the AI processing time.

---

## Tips

- **Save high-rarity artifacts immediately.** Pin legendary and epic artifacts from the detail view so they're easy to find later.
- **Use the Vault as a swipe file.** Even artifacts you don't use immediately are valuable references for future briefs.
- **Variations are underused.** The Variations artifact is often the most valuable — it shows the same brief approached from multiple angles, which is useful when the primary copy isn't quite right.
- **Hashtag Sets are reusable.** A strong Hashtag Set can be reused across multiple posts for the same campaign. Copy it from the Vault and add it to any post in the composer.
