# Getting Started with Marketer Pro Office

Welcome to Marketer Pro Office — your AI-powered marketing command center. This guide walks you through everything you need to know to go from zero to your first piece of published content.

---

## Step 1 — Create Your Account

1. Download **Marketer Pro Office** from the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android)
2. Open the app and tap **Create account**
3. Enter your email address and choose a strong password
4. Verify your email — check your inbox for a confirmation link
5. Once verified, tap **Access Desk** to enter for the first time

> **Already have an account?** Tap **Sign in** on the login screen.

---

## Step 2 — Complete Onboarding

The first time you open the app, a short onboarding sequence walks you through the Desk. It takes under 2 minutes and cannot be skipped on first launch. You can replay it any time from **Settings → Help → Replay Onboarding**.

What onboarding covers:
- An overview of the five Desk zones
- How to create your first brand
- How to trigger the Vault Generator for the first time

---

## Step 3 — Create Your First Brand

A **Brand** is the identity that shapes everything the AI generates — from the tone of copy to the visual palette.

1. From the Desk, tap the **brand name** in the top-left header (it will say "Expert Marketer's Desk" until you create one)
2. Tap **+ New Brand**
3. Enter your brand name
4. Tap **Open Brand Identity Chamber** to set your logo, colours, and tone — or tap **Skip for now** to do this later

> **Tip:** The more detail you give the Brand Identity Chamber, the better your AI-generated content will be. Don't skip it.

---

## Step 4 — Define Your Audience

Once your brand is set up, define who you're talking to in the **Audience Arena**:

1. Tap the **Tools** panel (left side of the Desk, or the panel icon in the bottom bar on mobile)
2. Tap **Audiences**
3. Tap traits that describe your target audience — demographics, psychographics, and behaviours
4. Tap **Lock Audience Intel** when done

---

## Step 5 — Generate Your First Content

With a brand and audience in place, you're ready to fire the Vault Generator:

1. Tap the **Generate** button (the gold wand icon in the centre of the bottom bar)
2. The **Generator Form** appears — select your platforms, content type, and write a brief goal
3. Tap **Fire the Reactor**
4. The cinematic sequence plays — Vault → Reactor → Presentation Chamber
5. Your AI-generated artifacts appear in the Presentation Chamber — select one to use

> **What are artifacts?** Artifacts are the outputs of a generation — copy, captions, hashtag sets, and variations. They're saved to your [Artifact Vault](./06-artifact-vault.md) automatically.

---

## Step 6 — Schedule and Publish

1. From the Presentation Chamber, tap **Schedule** on your chosen artifact
2. The **Scheduler Tower** opens — tap **Auto-Schedule** for the optimal time or choose your own
3. Confirm the time and tap **Confirm Schedule**
4. Your post appears in the **Calendar Drawer** (right side of the Desk)

> To actually publish to social platforms, you need to connect them first. See [Social Integrations](./07-social-integrations.md).

---

## What Happens Next

As you generate content and complete tasks, you earn **XP** and level up. Higher levels unlock:
- New VR scenes
- Office visual upgrades
- Lootbox rewards
- Advanced tools and templates

See [Progression & Rewards](./08-progression-rewards.md) for the full breakdown.

## Quick Reference — Desk Layout

| Zone | Where | What it does |
|---|---|---|
| **Workspace Header** | Top bar | Brand switcher, level badge, calendar toggle, notifications |
| **Tools Panel** | Left side | Navigation to all tools and scenes |
| **Planner Surface** | Centre | Today's tasks, completion tracking |
| **Calendar Drawer** | Right side | Monthly calendar, scheduled posts |
| **Quick Actions Bar** | Bottom | Generate, New Post, Campaign, Calendar shortcuts |
