Cookie & Storage Policy

Effective Date: May 27, 2026 · Last Updated: May 27, 2026

The short version: MarketerPro does not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or analytics cookies. We only use essential browser storage required for the app to function. No cookie consent banner is needed.

1. What We Use

MarketerPro uses browser localStorage (not cookies) for the following essential purposes:

Storage KeyTypePurposeDurationEssential?
Authentication token (JWT)localStorageKeeps you logged in between sessions. Without this, you'd need to re-enter your password on every page load.Until logout or token expiryYes
User preferenceslocalStorageRemembers your last-viewed screen, theme preference, and UI state.Until cleared or account deletedYes
Onboarding progresslocalStorageTracks which onboarding steps you've completed so you don't have to repeat them.Until onboarding completeYes

4. Why No Cookie Banner?

Under GDPR (EU), ePrivacy Directive, CCPA (California), and LGPD (Brazil), cookie consent banners are only required for non-essential cookies — tracking, analytics, and advertising cookies. Since MarketerPro uses only essential storage required for the service to function, no consent banner is legally required.

If we ever add non-essential cookies or third-party analytics in the future, we will:

  1. Update this policy at least 30 days before the change
  2. Implement a proper cookie consent mechanism with opt-in (not opt-out) for non-essential cookies
  3. Notify you via email and in-app notification

5. How to Clear Storage

  • Web: Open browser Developer Tools > Application tab > Local Storage > Clear. Or simply log out — the authentication token is removed.
  • iOS: Delete the app to clear all local data including Keychain entries for MarketerPro.
  • Android: Settings > Apps > MarketerPro > Clear Data. Or delete the app.

Clearing local storage will log you out but will not delete your account or server-side data. To delete all data, use Settings > Delete Account within the app.
