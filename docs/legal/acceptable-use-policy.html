Acceptable Use Policy

Effective Date: May 27, 2026 · Last Updated: May 27, 2026

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") governs how you may use MarketerPro, operated by Adiboy Advertising Co. This AUP is part of our Terms of Service. Violations may result in account suspension or termination.

1. General Principles

MarketerPro is a professional marketing tool built for businesses and solopreneurs. You agree to use the Service responsibly, ethically, and in compliance with all applicable laws and platform terms of service.

2. Prohibited Content

You may not use MarketerPro to generate, store, or publish content that:

2.1 Illegal Content

  • Violates any applicable federal, state, local, or international law
  • Promotes illegal activities, including drug trafficking, weapons sales, or human trafficking
  • Constitutes fraud, phishing, or social engineering
  • Infringes on any third party's intellectual property, copyright, trademark, or trade secret

2.2 Harmful Content

  • Contains hate speech targeting race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or national origin
  • Promotes violence, terrorism, or self-harm
  • Contains sexually explicit material or content exploiting minors
  • Constitutes bullying, harassment, or threats
  • Spreads disinformation or deliberately misleading health, safety, or election information

2.3 Deceptive Content

  • Impersonates another person, brand, or organization
  • Creates fake testimonials, reviews, or endorsements
  • Misrepresents the nature, origin, or source of products or services
  • Uses AI-generated content to deceive audiences about whether content is human-created (where such disclosure is required by law or platform policy)

3. Prohibited Behaviors

3.1 Spam & Abuse

  • Sending unsolicited bulk content or messages
  • Creating multiple accounts to circumvent rate limits or plan restrictions
  • Using Autonomous Mode to flood platforms with low-quality, repetitive content
  • Artificially inflating engagement metrics

3.2 Platform Violations

  • Publishing content that violates Instagram, Facebook, X, LinkedIn, or TikTok's community guidelines or terms of service
  • Using MarketerPro to circumvent platform bans, suspensions, or content restrictions
  • Connecting accounts you are not authorized to manage

3.3 Security Violations

  • Attempting to access other users' accounts or data
  • Circumventing authentication, access controls, or rate limits
  • Reverse engineering, decompiling, or disassembling the Service
  • Introducing malware, viruses, or malicious code
  • Scraping, crawling, or automated data extraction from the Service
  • Attempting to probe, scan, or test vulnerabilities without written authorization

3.4 Resource Abuse

  • Using AI generation features in ways designed to exhaust API quotas (prompt stuffing, rapid-fire requests)
  • Using the Service as a general-purpose AI chatbot rather than for marketing content
  • Storing non-marketing content in the Service to exploit storage allocations

4. Autonomous Mode Responsibilities

When you enable Autonomous Mode, you remain responsible for all content published on your behalf. AI acts within the guardrails you set, but you must:
  • Review your brand profile and ensure it accurately represents your business before enabling Autonomous Mode
  • Monitor notifications — MarketerPro notifies you when each autonomous post goes live
  • Pause or cancel autonomous runs if you notice content that doesn't meet your standards
  • Respond promptly to "needs your attention" notifications (user-only decision points)
  • Not use Autonomous Mode to generate content at volumes that would violate platform rate limits or spam policies

5. AI-Generated Content Disclosure

Several jurisdictions and platforms are implementing or considering AI disclosure requirements. You are responsible for:

  • Complying with any applicable laws requiring disclosure that content was AI-generated
  • Following platform-specific policies on AI-generated content labeling (e.g., Meta's AI content labels)
  • Not using AI-generated images to create realistic depictions of real people without their consent

6. Industry-Specific Restrictions

If you operate in a regulated industry, additional rules apply:

  • Healthcare: Do not generate content that constitutes medical advice. Include appropriate disclaimers.
  • Financial services: Do not generate content that constitutes financial advice or guarantees returns. Include required regulatory disclosures.
  • Legal: Do not generate content that constitutes legal advice.
  • Alcohol/Cannabis: Comply with age-gating and advertising restrictions in your jurisdiction.
  • Political: Comply with campaign finance disclosure and political advertising transparency laws.

7. Enforcement

Violations of this policy may result in:
  1. Warning: First-time minor violations — we'll notify you and explain what needs to change.
  2. Content removal: We may remove or prevent publishing of content that violates this policy.
  3. Feature restriction: Autonomous Mode or publishing access may be temporarily disabled.
  4. Account suspension: Repeated or serious violations — temporary suspension with notice.
  5. Account termination: Severe violations (illegal content, security attacks) — immediate termination without refund.

We aim to be fair and proportionate. Minor, unintentional violations are typically resolved with a warning. We will always explain what was violated and give you the opportunity to correct the issue before escalating (except in cases of illegal content or active security threats).

8. Reporting Violations

If you believe another user is violating this policy, or if you've inadvertently violated it yourself, contact us at abuse@adiboyads.com. We investigate all reports and respond within 5 business days.

9. Changes

We may update this AUP from time to time. Material changes are communicated via email and in-app notification at least 30 days before they take effect.
