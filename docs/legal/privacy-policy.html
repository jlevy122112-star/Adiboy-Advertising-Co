Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 27, 2026 · Last Updated: May 27, 2026

Adiboy Advertising Co. ("we," "us," "our") operates MarketerPro (the "Service"), an AI-powered marketing content generator, planner, and social media scheduler. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service across all platforms — web, Android (Google Play Store), iOS (Apple App Store), Amazon Appstore, and Microsoft Store.

Our commitment: You always have a say in every decision. MarketerPro operates on four control modes — you choose how much AI does and how much you do yourself. This extends to your data: you control what we store, what we access, and what we publish on your behalf.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Name, email address, and password (hashed with bcrypt — never stored in plaintext)
  • Business name, industry, and brand information you provide during our 5-step onboarding journey
  • Brand personality (3 words), problem/solution/outcome statements, and competitor references
  • Contact information: phone, email, website, social handles, physical address (all optional)
  • Billing information (processed and stored exclusively by Stripe, Inc. — we never store full card numbers)

1.2 Social Media Credentials (Two-Token Model)

When you connect social accounts (Instagram, Facebook, X/Twitter, LinkedIn, TikTok), MarketerPro uses a two-token hybrid architecture (ADR-001) designed for maximum security and compliance across all app stores:

Token TypeStoragePurposeYour Control
Device TokeniOS Keychain / Android Keystore (on-device only)Immediate, manual posting while app is openRevoke any time in Settings > Connected Platforms
Server Scheduling TokenEncrypted at rest in our database (AES-256)Scheduled posts and Autonomous Mode (background publishing)Separate opt-in consent; disable independently without disconnecting the platform

Explicit consent flow: After each platform OAuth, a second screen asks whether to enable background posting. You can choose "Enable Background Posting" (server token created) or "Not Now" (device-only — manual posting only). This choice is reversible at any time.

What we request: MarketerPro requests only publish and read permissions. We never request access to your DMs, contacts, or private data beyond what's needed to post on your behalf.

1.3 Content You Create

We store the marketing content generated through the Service, including:

  • Post copy, hashtags, and metadata for each of our supported platforms
  • AI-generated images (uploaded to Amazon S3 with server-side encryption)
  • AI-generated video scripts (text-only — no video files)
  • Campaign plans and post schedules
  • Brand profiles, voice settings, and brand memory context
  • Approval/rejection decisions and edit history per post

1.4 Usage Data

We automatically collect:

  • Device type, browser, operating system, and app version
  • IP address and approximate location (city/region level — never precise GPS)
  • Pages visited, features used, and session duration
  • Error logs and performance metrics
  • In-app search history and generation parameters

1.5 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your social media followers, following lists, or DMs
  • We do not collect contacts or address book data
  • We do not collect precise geolocation
  • We do not use fingerprinting or cross-app tracking

4. Data Storage & Security

  • All data in transit encrypted via HTTPS/TLS 1.3
  • Passwords hashed with bcrypt (never stored in plaintext)
  • OAuth tokens: device tokens in OS keychain/keystore; server tokens encrypted at rest (AES-256)
  • AI-generated images stored in S3 with server-side encryption and immutable cache headers
  • Database access restricted by role-based permissions
  • JWT-based authentication with short-lived tokens (HS256)
  • All API keys and secrets stored in environment variables — never in frontend code (Build Guardrail Rule 7)

6. Your Rights

6.1 All Users

  • Access — Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification — Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Erasure — Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability — Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format (JSON)
  • Disconnect — Revoke any connected social platform from Settings > Connected Platforms at any time
  • Account deletion — Delete your entire account from Settings (required by Apple App Store Guidelines)

6.2 EU/EEA Residents (GDPR)

  • Restriction — Limit how we process your data
  • Objection — Object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Withdraw consent — Where processing is based on consent
  • Lodge a complaint — With your local supervisory authority

6.3 California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

  • Right to Know: What personal information we collect and how it's used
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale: We do not sell your personal information. Period.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not treat you differently for exercising your rights
  • Right to Correct: Request correction of inaccurate personal information

6.4 How to Exercise Your Rights

Email privacy@adiboyads.com with your request. We verify your identity and respond within 30 days (45 days for complex requests, with notice). Account deletion is also available directly in-app via Settings.

7. Children's Privacy

MarketerPro is a business tool intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 18. If we learn we have collected data from a minor, we will delete it within 72 hours of discovery. If you believe a minor has provided us personal data, contact privacy@adiboyads.com.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in the United States where our servers are located. For EU/EEA users, transfers are protected by Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) as approved by the European Commission. For UK users, we rely on the UK International Data Transfer Addendum.
