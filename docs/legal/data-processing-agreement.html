Data Processing Agreement

Effective Date: May 27, 2026 · Last Updated: May 27, 2026

This Data Processing Agreement ("DPA") forms part of the Terms of Service between Adiboy Advertising Co. ("Processor," "we," "us") and the customer ("Controller," "you") using MarketerPro (the "Service"). This DPA applies where we process personal data on your behalf in the course of providing the Service.

1. Definitions

  • "Personal Data" means any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person, as defined by GDPR Article 4(1) and equivalent laws.
  • "Processing" means any operation performed on personal data, including collection, storage, use, disclosure, and deletion.
  • "Sub-processor" means any third party engaged by us to process personal data on your behalf.
  • "Data Subject" means the individual whose personal data is processed.
  • "Supervisory Authority" means the data protection authority with jurisdiction over the Controller.

2. Scope of Processing

2.1 Subject Matter

We process personal data solely to provide the MarketerPro Service — AI content generation, campaign planning, scheduling, and social media publishing.

2.2 Categories of Data Subjects

  • The Controller's employees and authorized users of the Service
  • The Controller's customers or audience members whose data appears in brand profiles or campaign targeting

2.4 Duration

Processing continues for the duration of the service agreement. Upon termination, we delete all personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

3. Obligations of the Processor

3.1 Lawful Processing

We process personal data only on your documented instructions. We will not process personal data for any purpose other than providing the Service unless required by applicable law, in which case we will inform you before processing (unless prohibited by law).

3.2 Confidentiality

All personnel with access to personal data are bound by confidentiality obligations. Access is restricted on a need-to-know basis using role-based permissions.

3.5 Data Subject Rights

We assist you in fulfilling data subject requests (access, rectification, erasure, portability, restriction, objection) by:

  • Providing account data export on request (JSON format)
  • Deleting personal data within 30 days of a verified request
  • Revoking OAuth tokens immediately upon disconnect request
  • Supporting in-app account deletion (Settings > Delete Account)

3.6 Data Breach Notification

In the event of a personal data breach, we will:

  1. Notify you without undue delay and no later than 72 hours after becoming aware of the breach
  2. Provide details of the breach: nature, categories and approximate number of data subjects affected, likely consequences, and measures taken to mitigate
  3. Cooperate with you in notifying the relevant supervisory authority and affected data subjects where required
  4. Document the breach and remediation actions taken

3.7 Audit Rights

You have the right to audit our compliance with this DPA. We will:

  • Make available all information necessary to demonstrate compliance
  • Allow and contribute to audits conducted by you or an auditor you appoint (with reasonable advance notice)
  • Immediately inform you if we believe an instruction from you violates GDPR or other applicable data protection law

4. International Transfers

Personal data may be transferred to and processed in the United States. For transfers from the EU/EEA, we rely on:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) — Commission Implementing Decision (EU) 2021/914, Module Two (Controller to Processor)
  • UK International Data Transfer Addendum — for transfers from the United Kingdom

We conduct transfer impact assessments and implement supplementary measures where required.

5. Data Deletion & Return

Upon termination of the Service or upon your request:

  • All personal data deleted within 30 days
  • All OAuth tokens (server scheduling tokens) revoked and deleted immediately
  • All AI-generated images purged from S3 within 30 days
  • Confirmation of deletion provided upon request
  • Exception: data retained where required by applicable law (e.g., billing records for 7 years)

6. Liability

Each party's liability under this DPA is subject to the limitations set forth in the Terms of Service. This DPA does not limit either party's liability for breaches of data protection law where such limitation is not permitted.

7. Governing Law

This DPA is governed by the same governing law as the Terms of Service. For matters related to GDPR, the laws of the EU Member State of the Controller shall apply to the extent required by GDPR.
