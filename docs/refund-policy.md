# Refund Policy

**Effective date:** 1 January 2026
**Last updated:** 1 January 2026

---

## 1. Overview

Marketer Pro Office offers free and paid subscription plans. This Refund Policy explains how refunds work depending on how you subscribed.

---

## 2. Subscriptions via Apple App Store

If you subscribed through the Apple App Store, your purchase is governed by Apple's refund policy. Marketer Pro Office does not process refunds for App Store purchases directly.

**To request a refund from Apple:**
1. Visit [reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com)
2. Sign in with your Apple ID
3. Find the subscription charge and select "Request a Refund"
4. Choose a reason and submit

Apple typically processes refund requests within 48 hours. Refunds are at Apple's sole discretion under their Media Services Terms and Conditions.

**Cancelling your subscription:**
Go to **Settings (iPhone) → Your Name → Subscriptions → Marketer Pro Office → Cancel Subscription**. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period. You retain access to Pro features until the period ends.

---

## 3. Subscriptions via Google Play

If you subscribed through Google Play, your purchase is governed by Google's refund policy. Marketer Pro Office does not process refunds for Google Play purchases directly.

**To request a refund from Google:**
1. Visit [play.google.com/store/account/subscriptions](https://play.google.com/store/account/subscriptions)
2. Select the Marketer Pro Office subscription
3. Tap "Manage" → "Cancel subscription" and follow the prompts to also request a refund

Google may offer a full refund within 48 hours of the original purchase for new subscriptions. After that window, refunds are at Google's discretion.

**Cancelling your subscription:**
Go to **Google Play Store → Menu → Subscriptions → Marketer Pro Office → Cancel**. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.

---

## 4. Direct / Web Subscriptions (Enterprise)

For Enterprise plans billed directly by Marketer Pro Office (outside the App Store and Google Play):

### 4.1 Monthly plans
We offer a **pro-rata refund** for the unused portion of the current billing month if you cancel within 7 days of your renewal date and contact us at billing@marketerprooffice.com.

### 4.2 Annual plans
We offer a **pro-rata refund** for the unused full months remaining on your annual plan if you cancel within 30 days of purchase or renewal and contact us at billing@marketerprooffice.com. No refund is offered after 30 days.

### 4.3 How to request
Email **billing@marketerprooffice.com** with:
- Your account email address
- Your subscription plan and billing date
- The reason for your cancellation

We will process approved refunds within 10 business days via the original payment method.

---

## 5. Free Trial

If you have an active free trial and cancel before it ends, you will not be charged. No refund is applicable because no charge has been made.

---

## 6. Non-Refundable Items

The following are non-refundable in all circumstances:

- Subscription periods that have already elapsed
- XP boosts or one-time cosmetic purchases (if applicable in future releases)
- Accounts terminated for violation of our Terms of Use or Acceptable Use Policy

---

## 7. Service Disruptions

If Marketer Pro Office experiences a significant service outage (defined as the core content generation feature being unavailable for more than 24 consecutive hours) within your current billing period, you may be eligible for a partial credit. Contact **support@marketerprooffice.com** with details. Credits are applied to your next billing period and are not issued as cash refunds.

---

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here with an updated effective date. For significant changes, we will provide in-app notice at least 14 days in advance.
